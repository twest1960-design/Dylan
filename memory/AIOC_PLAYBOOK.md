# AIOC Playbook

## 1. Overview

The Alberta Indigenous Opportunities Corporation (AIOC) provides loan guarantees, not direct loans.

Its mandate is to support Indigenous participation in revenue-generating infrastructure and business projects across multiple sectors, including energy, infrastructure, and agriculture.

Projects must demonstrate:

- Strong, predictable cash flow  
- Long-term economic value  
- Meaningful Indigenous ownership  
- Low to moderate risk  

---

## 2. Core Principle

AIOC evaluates one primary question:

**Can this project reliably service debt over time?**

Everything else is secondary.

---

## 3. Eligible Sectors

AIOC supports:

- Energy and natural resources  
- Infrastructure and transportation  
- Utilities and power  
- Agriculture and food production  
- Industrial and processing facilities  

Agriculture is eligible when structured as a cashflow-supported business.

---

## 4. What AIOC Provides

AIOC provides:

- Loan guarantees  
- Risk reduction for lenders  
- Credibility for structured deals  

---

## 5. Investment Criteria

### Cash Flow
- Immediate or near-term revenue preferred  
- Stable and predictable income  
- Contracted or reliable sales  

### Debt Service
- Ability to meet loan obligations  
- Conservative assumptions  

### Indigenous Participation
- Ownership and long-term benefit  

### Risk Profile
- Proven model  
- Strong operators  

---

## 6. What Does NOT Work

- Greenfield without revenue  
- Speculative projects  
- Projection-based models  
- No clear repayment path  

---

## 7. What DOES Work

- Operating assets  
- Acquisition strategies  
- Proven revenue  
- Offtake agreements  
- Strong partners  

---

## 8. Capacity Grants

Capacity funding may support:

- Due diligence  
- Feasibility  
- Engineering  
- Financial modeling  

---

## 9. Strategic Positioning

Position as:

**Cashflow-backed infrastructure investment**

Not:

- Startup  
- Concept  
- Growth story  

---

## 10. Example Structure

- Moe’s → revenue  
- Mrs. G’s → growth  
- Roman → expertise  
- District 20 → ownership  
- AIOC → guarantee  

---

## 11. Key Insight

AIOC is evaluating:

**Is this bankable?**

---

## 12. Language to Use

Use:

- Cashflow-supported  
- Debt service  
- Structured investment  

Avoid:

- Startup  
- Potential  
- Upside  

---

## 13. Final Takeaway

Structure first  
Revenue first  
Risk reduction first  

---

## 14. Closing

The goal is to fit AIOC’s model, not change it.
